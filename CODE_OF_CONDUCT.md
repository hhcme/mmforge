# Code Of Conduct

MMForge aims to be a focused, respectful, technically serious open-source community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Explain trade-offs clearly.
- Assume good intent while still being precise.
- Make room for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion.
- Intentionally submitting malicious code, data, or misleading license information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, or other project spaces to keep the community healthy and technically useful.

## 中文说明

MMForge 希望保持一个尊重、聚焦、认真讨论技术的开源社区。

请针对想法和代码提出意见，不要攻击个人；清楚说明技术取舍；尊重不同背景和经验水平的贡献者。骚扰、威胁、歧视、公开他人隐私、恶意提交代码或误导性许可证信息都不可接受。
